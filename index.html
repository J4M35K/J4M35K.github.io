Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a party?

To start a party, simply press the Host button and create an account. After creating an account, you'll be prompted to sign in with Spotify. Once signed in, pick a song to play, create a party name, and share it with all your friends!

How do I join a party?

To join a party, simply join as a guest and ask the person hosting the party for the party name. Once you have the party name, you can join the party!

Why does the current song in my app differ from what I'm hearing?

Sometimes, in areas with poor network connectivity, our app may take a bit longer to update. If you're still experiencing discrepancies even after a song change, please contact us via our form, and we'll promptly assist you.
